# Business Dynamics — Hypotheses, Strategy & Processes (Bundle 1)

## Overview

Bundle 1 defines **how the product evolves, adapts, and operates in reality over time**.

It transforms:

- uncertainty → knowledge
- knowledge → decisions
- decisions → strategy
- strategy → execution

This bundle introduces **dynamics, change, and real execution**, building on the static foundation defined in Bundle 0.
