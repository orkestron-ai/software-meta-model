# Bundle 1 — Business Dynamics (Hypotheses, Strategy & Processes)

## Overview

Bundle 1 defines **how the product evolves and operates over time**.

It answers:

- What assumptions are we testing?
- Why are we changing the product?
- What strategic direction are we following?
- What initiatives and roadmap items are planned?
- How does the product actually operate through processes?

This bundle introduces **dynamics, change, and execution**, building on the static foundation defined in Bundle 0.

---

## Layers Overview

### 101 — Business Hypotheses
Defines structured uncertainty and assumptions:
- hypotheses
- assumptions
- expected outcomes
- validation logic
- evidence and decisions

---

### 102 — Strategy and Product Management
Defines product direction and planning:
- strategy
- objectives
- initiatives
- roadmap
- product versions
- prioritization

---

### 103 — Processes
Defines real operational workflows:
- business processes
- steps, events, and roles
- process hierarchy
- connections between processes

---

## Role of Bundle 1

Bundle 1 provides a coherent model:

```
Hypotheses → Strategy → Processes
```

It is used as:

- driver of product evolution
- bridge between ideas and execution
- alignment layer between business and operations
- foundation for requirements and implementation

---

## Key Principles

- Change starts with hypotheses, not tasks
- Strategy is derived from validated knowledge
- Processes reflect real execution, not idealized flows
- Strong traceability between layers
- Clear separation from static product definition

---

## Summary

Bundle 1 defines **how the product changes, why it changes, and how it operates in reality**.

It connects uncertainty, decision-making, and execution into a single dynamic model.
